How to Play

Deal cards one at a time by tapping the deck. When the newest card matches the card three positions back:

Suit match The two middle cards are discarded.
Rank match All four cards are discarded.

Clear all 52 cards to win. The odds are approximately 1 in 142.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new game?

Tap the "New" button at the bottom of the screen.

Where are my statistics stored?

All statistics are stored locally on your device. They are not synced to the cloud or shared with anyone.

Can I reset my statistics?

Currently, statistics cannot be reset. This feature may be added in a future update.

Why do my statistics show games I didn't finish?

A game is counted when you start it. If you tap "New" before finishing, the incomplete game is recorded with your score at that point.

Is there sound?

Yes. Make sure your device is not in silent mode. You can adjust sound volume or disable sounds in the Menu.
