Railroad! Solitaire

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Data We Collect

Leslie Street Studio does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game statistics (games played, wins, scores) are stored locally on your device and are never shared with the developer.

The app includes Google AdMob for advertising (see below). AdMob may collect certain data as described in Google's privacy policy, including device identifiers, IP address, and usage data for ad delivery and measurement purposes.

Analytics

This app does not use any first-party analytics or tracking services.

Advertising

Railroad! Solitaire displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS), IP address, and diagnostic data to serve and measure ads. If you have not granted app tracking permission on your device, AdMob will serve non-personalized ads only.

The app also offers optional rewarded ads — you may choose to watch an ad in exchange for an in-game reward. This option is available to all players, including those who have purchased ad removal. Because rewarded ads are voluntarily initiated, AdMob's data practices apply whenever a rewarded ad is viewed.

You can review Google's data practices at: policies.google.com/privacy

In-App Purchases

Railroad! Solitaire offers a one-time purchase to remove non-optional ads. Players who purchase ad removal will no longer see banner or interstitial ads, but may still choose to watch rewarded ads voluntarily. All transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Leslie Street Studio does not receive or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs the handling of purchase data.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact: support@lesliestreetstudio.com

Changes

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page.
