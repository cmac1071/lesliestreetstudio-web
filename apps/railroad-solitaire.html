How it works

A game of alignment and chain reactions

52 cards are dealt one by one into a single row. Your success depends entirely on the relationship between the 1st and 4th cards from the right end of the row.

Rank match

Same rank — clear four

When the 1st and 4th cards from the right share the same rank — two Kings, two Fives — all four cards in that group are removed from the row.

Suit match

Same suit — clear two

When the 1st and 4th cards share the same suit — both Hearts, both Clubs — the two middle cards are removed. The outer cards remain.

The cascade. Every time cards are removed, the row collapses and new matches can form — triggering chain reactions that clear large sections at once. Win by clearing all 52 cards.
