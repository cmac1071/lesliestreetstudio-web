02

How It Works

Four steps. One perfect pour.

1

Scan Your Shelf

Open BarVision and point your iPhone at your spirits collection. Tap once to capture, or use pan mode to slowly sweep across a wide shelf. The AI reads every label.

Computer Vision · On-Device AI
2

Confirm Your Inventory

Review detected bottles, correct anything the camera missed, and mark fill levels. Your inventory lives privately on your device — no cloud, no account required.

On-Device Storage · Private by Design
3

See What You Can Make

Browse every cocktail you can make right now, what you're one bottle away from, and smart suggestions for what to buy next.

600+ Recipes · Offline Matching
4

Pour With Confidence

Tap any recipe for guided make mode. Follow each step with technique cues, built-in timers, and measurements that scale to your serving size.

Step-by-Step · Batch Scaling
